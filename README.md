Time of Day Plugin

A simple time and atmosphere plugin for Owlbear Rodeo.

This plugin helps you change the mood of your scene by adjusting the time of day, such as morning, daylight, sunset, night, moonlight, and other lighting states. It is designed to make scene atmosphere easier to control during tabletop sessions.

Features
Change the time of day in your scene
Create different lighting moods
Useful for outdoor maps, travel, camps, towns, dungeons, and night encounters
Helps make scenes feel more alive and atmospheric
Lightweight and easy to use
Purpose

The goal of this plugin is to make it quick and simple to show the passing of time and create a stronger visual mood during your games.
